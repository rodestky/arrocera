Arrocera San Fernando

Arrocera San Fernando is a company with over 100 years of experience serving the public sector. We are currently certified suppliers to the Chilean government through the "Public Market" portal. Through the logistics and coordination of three distribution centers, we have built a mercantile trajectory from Putre to Yendegaia, spanning the length of Chile and solidifying commercial relationships with our customers. Our most important clients include hospitals, armed forces, municipalities, schools, and universities.

We are a company dedicated to the importation of a wide range of food products, clothing, toiletries, and hardware. In the food sector, we deliver both human and animal food. Regarding human food, our products include rice, oil, legumes, fruits, vegetables, canned goods, meats, sweets, among others. For animal nutrition, we mainly supply alfalfa, oats, wheat, pellets, and pet food. We import directly from China, the USA, Canada, Sri Lanka, Turkey, Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, Peru, Germany, Pakistan, among others.

Arrocera San Fernando has earned the reputation of being one of the leading importing organizations of food products in Chile, meeting the highest quality standards, ensuring the commitment and trust provided by our customers. The company has an extensive network of contacts within and outside the country, due to the excellence of its products.

Arrocera San Fernando is now part of ACSB Limited, a shareholder group that bets on growth and commitment to national interests, with its main markets in the USA, China, and Chile. Among its commercial sectors, it stands out in clothing, construction, and food, which is why they acquired Arrocera San Fernando to expand their operations in Chile.

In the post-Covid era, our company faces a transformative challenge that transcends conventional boundaries. We are committed to leading not only in commercial terms but also as a positive agent of change in the San Fernando community and its surroundings. The redefinition of our goals has led us to take a bold step. In this new chapter, we prioritize the well-being and quality of life of our neighbors. To this end, we have meticulously selected a wide variety of the highest quality products, offering unprecedented prices, exclusively designed. Our mission goes beyond being a company; we strive to be a catalyst for hope and revitalization for those around us. In this renewed commitment to the community, we seek not only to provide exceptional products at affordable prices but also to establish a stronger and more meaningful connection with each individual. We strive to be a beacon of resilience and positivity in these challenging times, working tirelessly to overcome any obstacles that the new normal may present.
