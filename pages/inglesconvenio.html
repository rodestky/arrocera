Exploring Opportunities in the Public Market

In the vast universe of commercial transactions, Public Market emerges strongly, the platform managed by ChileCompra. This scenario, where more than 850 public entities make acquisitions, becomes the fertile ground where Arrocera San Fernando unfolds its commitment and excellence

Our presence on this platform not only encompasses products and services but translates into a constant delivery that crosses borders, bringing quality and dedication from the northernmost regions to the southernmost territories of the country

Framework Agreement

Framework Agreements are a purchasing modality that results in an electronic store where public buyers can acquire goods and services with just a click. For each Framework Agreement, ChileCompra conducts a public tender according to the criteria of the respective industries, selecting suppliers who then list their products in the store's catalog.

Framework Agreements: Collaborating in E-Commerce

Framework Agreements are a fabric where needs and solutions intertwine. Through public tenders, rigorously overseen by ChileCompra, Arrocera San Fernando stands as a prominent supplier, incorporating its products into the virtual catalog that simplifies the acquisition of goods and services with just one click

Agile Purchase: Facilitating Access to the Best

Agile Purchase, a model of agility and openness, allows public entities to access our products quickly through www.mercadopublico.cl. A window that, without restrictions, offers goods and services from Arrocera San Fernando for amounts equal to or less than 30 Monthly Tax Units.
